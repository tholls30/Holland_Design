SALIDO

The restaurant operating system.

Project overview

SALIDO is the Restaurant OS, a single platform that unifies your restaurant operations under one login.

The Problem

The goal of this project was to redesign our Time Cards feature to prepare for larger labor enhancements and an enhanced UX based on customer feedback. This was a challenge because the team had to think about the implementation of time cards wholistically as the data being collected in this tool had a large impact throughout the entire SALIDO system.

The Process

As I always do, I started out with research. This included understanding the desired business outcome; reviewing the previously implemented solution, requirements, and feedback from users; talking to various clients across different user groups about how their operations manage their employee’s time cards; observing as the particular users tasked with managing time cards on a daily or weekly basis used our tool; and researching what clients liked and disliked about our current time cards solution. After analyzing and synthesizing all the data into documentation I reviewed my findings with key stakeholders in the business and internal subject matter experts (SME) to elicit their feedback and brainstorm ideas. I also looked at our top competitors to see how they solved certain problems. I then used this feedback and information to create a UX map and built out the user workflows in preparation to create wireframes. There were also several white-boarding sessions revolving around particular pain points.

The Conclusion

After several iterations, design reviews, some more feedback and analysis the product manager and I were able to create a complete set of designs along with the appropriate workflows and business logic. We then did a final review of the designs and built out a prototype with InVision Studios to test externally with our clients. We received great feeback that confirmed we had solved the previously stated problems and were ready implent the new design.

Additional Work

During my time at SALIDO I lead an initiative to create a design system for both our web app and our iOS apps. I also worked on an employee onboarding wizard, comletely overhauled our advanced reporting interface, created a scheduling tool from scratch that integrated with the time cards tool, and worked on a manager app that allowed real-time tracking of data and operations.

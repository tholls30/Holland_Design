What I do

I am adept at web and application product design as well as developing strategies and brands for small businesses

Product Design

I have spent the past 8 years of my professional career working as a digital product designer both as an employee for various companies and on my own as a self-employed, freelance designer. As a creative design professional I have worked for companies of all sizes where I have designed iOS, Android, and Web Apps as well as websites, plug-ins, and enterprise software. Through all of these experiences I have collaborated with cross-functional teams to solve complex problems.

  • Web & Mobile Applications
  • Enterprise Software
  • UI/UX Design
  • Prototyping
  • Design Systems
  • Design Thinking

Product Management

My professional experience goes beyond solely product design as I continuously strive to broaden my skillset. Early on in my career I fulfilled the role of a product manager as well as a product designer and continued to exercise that skillset as a freelance designer in charge of the successfully delivery of each project I work on. I am constantly learning from those around me and stay up-to-date on the latest practices and trends in the field of product development.

  • Product Lifecycle Management
  • User Research & Testing
  • Product Documentation
  • Project Roadmaps
  • Team Management
  • Metric Tracking

Small Business Consulting

Through my experience with several start-up companies as well as launching my own business I have been able to learn about what it takes to start, manage, and develop a small business. I have also worked with others as they created or improved upon their own small businesses, offering advice on strategy, marketing, funding, and day-to-day operations.
